DESCRIPTION OF PROPERTY
Zolo Carnations in Electronic City Phase 2 offers hassle-free long-term stay for women, daily meals and wifi included. Choose from 2-sharing and single occupancy rooms that come with wide, wooden beds and branded comfortable mattresses. Every room has a TV, cupboard, attached bathroom etc. Common spaces provide the benefit of appliances (washing machine, microwave, fridge), community dining, open terrace to celebrate the weather and recreational room equipped with items of entertainment. Exclusive terrace rooms available on request. Situated in Narayana Reddy Layout, Zolo Carnations is suitable for women who work in and around Tech Mahindra, HP, Infosys, Schneider Electric, TCS, Fanuc, Tessolve etc. or are pursuing their studies in XIME, Symbiosis etc. Enjoy the advantage of Zolo being managed by a professional team and being powered by technology and a smart app.
